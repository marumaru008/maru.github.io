about us

Located in the beautiful city of Edmonton, Jumbo Dim Sum & Dining offers its respected guests fresh and authentic tasting Chinese cuisine. Our generous portions, quality meals, and friendly service is one of the many reasons our loyal customers keep coming back. To ensure every bite is a delicious experience for you, we use only the freshest ingredients.
